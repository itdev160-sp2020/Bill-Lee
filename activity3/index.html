Asus Laptop

Asus ROG Zephyrus S GX531GX

Asus ROG Zephyrus S GX531GX is a stunning 15-inch gaming laptop that has the svelte design of an ultrabook,
but packs powerful gaming components.

Specification

Powered by an 8th-generation Intel Core i7-8750H with six cores and 12 threads,
with a base frequency of 2.20GHz and a max turbo frequency of 4.10GHz.
This is Intel’s flagship mobile CPU, and it easily outclasses the processors
found in most laptops, and even in many desktop PCs.
The GX531GX features an Nvidia GeForce RTX 2080 with Max-Q design, which is Nvidia’s high-end mobile card.
It’s one of the most powerful gaming graphics cards ever included in a gaming laptop,
and the Max-Q design means it’s thinner than standard mobile GPUs.
